- You MUST NOT call `f` more than `budget` times. Track your evaluations.
- Dimensions range from 2 to 10.
- All coordinates are real-valued floats within the given bounds.
- No external libraries — pure Python only (math module is OK).
- The objective may have many local minima — your optimizer should escape them.
- Return the best point found, not the best value.

**HINT**: Consider strategies that balance exploration (searching broadly) with exploitation (refining promising regions). Random restarts, adaptive step sizes, or population-based approaches can help escape local minima.
